Lebanon Data Visualization Dashboard

Economic Indicators

This section presents key economic indicators for Lebanon based on World Bank data. The visualizations below show GDP, GDP per capita, GDP growth rates, and inflation trends over time, providing insights into Lebanon's economic performance and challenges.

GDP Trends

This chart shows Lebanon's Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in current US dollars over time. Note the significant decline after 2019 due to the financial crisis.

GDP per capita shows the average economic output per person and is an indicator of standard of living.

Annual GDP growth rate (%) illustrates economic expansion or contraction. Negative values represent economic recession.

Inflation

Lebanon's inflation rate shows the annual percent change in consumer prices. Note the hyperinflation following the 2019 financial crisis.

Data source: World Bank API (https://api.worldbank.org)
